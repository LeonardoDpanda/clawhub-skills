## Common Title Mistakes

❌ **Too Vague**
- "My Cooking Video" → "I Cooked Every Pasta Shape. Here's What I Learned."

❌ **Too Long**
- "In This Video I Will Show You How to Make the Best Pasta You've Ever Tasted in Your Life" → "The Secret to Restaurant-Quality Pasta at Home"

❌ **Clickbait Without Payoff**
- Promising something the video doesn't deliver

❌ **All Caps or Excessive Punctuation**
- "YOU WON'T BELIEVE THIS!!!" → "I Didn't Expect This to Happen"

❌ **Ignoring Mobile Viewers**
- Long titles get cut off on mobile devices

## Pro Tips

1. **Test 2-3 titles before publishing** - Use the optimizer to compare options
2. **Front-load important words** - Mobile viewers see only the first 40-50 characters
3. **Use brackets for context** - "(Free Template)" or "[2024 Edition]"
4. **Create curiosity, not confusion** - Be intriguing but clear
5. **Study your successful videos** - What titles worked before?

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will this guarantee my video goes viral?**
A: No tool can guarantee virality. But a great title significantly increases your chances by improving click-through rates.

A: Clickbait (misleading titles) hurts your channel long-term. Instead, use "curiosity gaps" - be intriguing but honest.
